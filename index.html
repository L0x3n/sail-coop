HOW TO SAIL

WASD move · mouse look (click or drag) · SPACE jump · E interact

Get going: step onto the boat, go to the bow and press E to raise the anchor, then man the HELM (wheel) and the SAIL (mast) with E. At a station, A/D steers / trims.

The wind: you can't sail straight into it — the compass shows a red no-go cone. Keep your bow out of it; zig-zag (tack) to work upwind.

The job: load crates from the home pier, sail to the green flag, stop & drop anchor, then carry crates onto the circle for gold.

Hands: F grab your matey (release = throw, mash F to escape). Mop: hold LMB scrub · tap LMB bonk · F throw.
